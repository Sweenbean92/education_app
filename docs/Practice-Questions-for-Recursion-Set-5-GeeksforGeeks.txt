fun(99) = fun(fun(110)) since 99 ? 100 = fun(100) since 110 > 100 = fun(fun(111)) since 100 ? 100 = fun(101) since 111 > 100 = 91 since 101 > 100

Time complexity: O(n)Auxiliary Space: O(n), to maintain function call into call stack
